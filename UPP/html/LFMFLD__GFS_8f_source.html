4 ! SUBPROGRAM: LFMFLD COMPUTES LAYER MEAN LFM FIELDS
5 ! PRGRMMR: TREADON ORG: W/NP2 DATE: 92-12-22
6 !
7 ! ABSTRACT:
8 ! THIS ROUTINE COMPUTES THREE LAYER MEAN RELATIVE HUMIDITIES
9 ! AND A PRECIPITABLE WATER FIELD FROM ETA LEVEL DATA. THE
10 ! COMPUTED FIELDS ARE INTENDED TO MIMIC SIMILAR FIELDS COM-
11 ! PUTED BY THE LFM. THE ALGORITHM USED HERE IS FAIRLY PRI-
12 ! MATIVE. IN EACH COLUMN ABOVE A MASS POINT ON THE ETA GRID
13 ! WE SET THE FOLLOWING TARGET PRESSURES:
14 ! SIGMA LAYER 1.00 PRESSURE: SURFACE PRESSURE
15 ! SIGMA LAYER 0.66 PRESSURE: 0.50 * SURFACE PRESSURE
16 ! SIGMA LAYER 0.33 PRESSURE: 0.4356 * SURFACE PRESSURE
17 ! GIVEN THESE PRESSURES A SURFACE UP SUMMATION IS MADE OF
18 ! RELATIVE HUMIDITY AND/OR PRECIPITABLE WATER BETWEEN THESE
19 ! TARGET PRESSURES. EACH TERM IN THE SUMMATION IS WEIGHTED
20 ! BY THE THICKNESS OF THE ETA LAYER. THE FINAL LAYER MEAN
21 ! IS THIS SUM NORMALIZED BY THE TOTAL DEPTH OF THE LAYER.
22 ! THERE IS, OBVIOUSLY, NO NORMALIZATION FOR PRECIPITABLE WATER.
23 !
24 !
25 ! PROGRAM HISTORY LOG:
26 ! 92-12-22 RUSS TREADON
27 ! 93-07-27 RUSS TREADON - MODIFIED SUMMATION LIMITS FROM
28 ! 0.66*PSFC TO 0.75*PSFC AND 0.33*PSFC
29 ! TO 0.50*PSFC, WHERE PSFC IS THE
30 ! SURFACES PRESSURE. THE REASON FOR
31 ! THIS CHANGE WAS RECOGNITION THAT IN
32 ! THE LFM 0.33 AND 0.66 WERE MEASURED
33 ! FROM THE SURFACE TO THE TROPOPAUSE,
34 ! NOT THE TOP OF THE MODEL.
35 ! 93-09-13 RUSS TREADON - RH CALCULATIONS WERE MADE INTERNAL
36 ! TO THE ROUTINE.
37 ! 96-03-04 MIKE BALDWIN - CHANGE PW CALC TO INCLUDE CLD WTR
38 ! 98-06-16 T BLACK - CONVERSION FROM 1-D TO 2-D
39 ! 98-08-17 MIKE BALDWIN - COMPUTE RH OVER ICE
40 ! 98-12-22 MIKE BALDWIN - BACK OUT RH OVER ICE
41 ! 00-01-04 JIM TUCCILLO - MPI VERSION
42 ! 02-04-24 MIKE BALDWIN - WRF VERSION
43 ! 06-11-06 H CHUANG - MODIFY TO OUTPUT GFS LFM FIELDS WHICH
44 ! HAVE DIFFERENT THICKNESS AS MESO AND USE DP
45 ! RATHER THAN DZ
46 !
47 !
48 ! USAGE: CALL LFMFLD(RH3310,RH6610,RH3366,PW3310)

50 ! NONE
